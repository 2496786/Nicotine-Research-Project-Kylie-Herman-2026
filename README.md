# [Nicotine-Research-Project-Kylie-Herman-2026][link]
[link]: https://2496786.github.io/Nicotine-Research-Project-Kylie-Herman-2026/

This project examines the chemical properties of nicotine, its effects on the body and the mechanisms of its addictive properties. It researches and analyzes data on how nicotine interacts with the human nervous system, its short- and long-term health consequences, and the public health issues associated with adolescent tobacco and vape use.
